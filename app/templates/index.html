Generate Trump Tweet

The Trump Tweet Machine was created to give voters and spectators a window into the minds of the candidates for president of the United States through their tweets.

It uses a Markov chain generator to create new tweets in the style of each candidate by pulling words from original tweets. Users can see the original text and a collage of images returned by a google search for each tweet referenced, as well as a plot of the candidate's use of each word on Twitter.
